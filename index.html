About Me

I am a Medical Doctor with multidisciplinary clinical experience across general practice, emergency medicine, pediatrics, and obstetrics in resource-limited settings. My work focuses on integrating clinical medicine with digital health, computational modeling, and systems-level thinking to improve diagnostic capacity and healthcare delivery. I have a strong interest in computational psychiatry, in-silico medicine, and the development of scalable tools that enhance clinical decision-making and access to care in underserved regions.
